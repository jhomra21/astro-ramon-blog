Cloudflare has introduced powerful AI tools that enable developers to integrate artificial intelligence capabilities directly into their applications at the edge. In this post, we'll explore the main offerings: Cloudflare Workers AI and AI Gateway.

## Cloudflare Workers AI

Cloudflare Workers AI is a platform that allows you to run machine learning models directly on Cloudflare's edge network. This means you can execute AI inference closer to your users, resulting in faster response times and lower latency.

### Key Features

- **Edge Execution**: Run AI models on Cloudflare's global network of data centers
- **Pre-trained Models**: Access to a variety of optimized models for common tasks
- **Simple Integration**: Easy to implement with Workers
- **Low Latency**: Models run close to your users

### Available Models

Cloudflare Workers AI supports various models for different tasks:

1. **Text Generation**
   - Deepseek R1

2. **Image Generation**
   - Flux




Cloudflare AI Tools provide a powerful platform for integrating AI capabilities into your applications. With Workers AI and AI Gateway, you can build sophisticated AI-powered applications that run at the edge, providing fast and cost-effective solutions for your users.
